About me

Alexander is a software engineer (BSc) with years of experience in creating ERD system solutions for national and international film distribution companies at a company called MACCS International.

In the first period of his career, he has helped the company by creating new functionalities and modules written in PowerScript (PowerBuilder). During this period, he started taking lead in projects, and did some consultancy, implementations, and training at film distribution companies in both the Netherlands and abroad (mainly in Europe).

During the second period, he led a team of developers to maintain and evolve the Maccs application. After several years of managing a team, he decided to go back to University at the age of 38 to gain new knowledge and insights in the field of software engineering.
