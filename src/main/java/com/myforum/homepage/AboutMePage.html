About me

Alexander is a software engineer (BSc) with years of experience in creating system solutions for national and international film distribution companies at a company called MACCS International. This includes, but is not limited to solutions for sales, finance, and logistics.

In the first period of his career, he started as a software developer working on both maintenance and new enhancements to the enterprise application, and quickly grew to taking lead in projects, setup Maccs at new clients both in the Netherlands and abroad (mainly in Europe), and training the end users.

During the second period, he became team lead for a group of developers to maintain and evolve the Maccs application, which quickly evolved into a management role. Because of the nature of this role, having to deal more with politics than actual programming, he felt he was losing touch with his original passion developing software. He decided to go back to University at the age of 38 to gain new knowledge and insights in the field of software engineering.

Today, he is working on Java projects that are mainly based on service-oriented-architecture solutions.
